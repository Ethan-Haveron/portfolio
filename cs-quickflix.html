QuickFlix

Overview

Content is changing, and how we consume content is changing. my task was to develop a micro-publication to express a less boring way to consume content and how I as a designer and creator must have content first before designing.

My Idea

The idea was called quick flicks and it was a mobile application game for movie enthusiasts to test their skill and guess a quote from a movie.

Project Time Frame

This was a small project for me as it was an exercise to get me thinking about the importance of content so thus the time frame was just over one week to get an idea and come up with a visual concept to show.

Personal Thoughts

I loved this project! it was a fantastic way to get my creative juices flowing. I want to make this more than just an idea. I want to make a fully functional game, currently, in my spare time, I am developing the idea further as much as I can due ti the positive feedback
